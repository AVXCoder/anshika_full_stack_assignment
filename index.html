The Process

Three simple steps to compete and contribute

01

Enter Your Scores

Log your last 5 Stableford scores (1–45 range). The system keeps a rolling record — new entries replace your oldest score automatically.

02

Enter the Monthly Draw

Your scores automatically enter you into the monthly prize draw. Match 3, 4, or 5 numbers to win a share of the prize pool.

03

Support a Charity

Choose a charity at signup. A minimum 10% of your subscription goes directly to your chosen cause — increase it any time you like.
